Air Pollution

Causes of Air Pollution

  • Burning fossil fuels (coal, oil, natural gas)
  • Industrial emissions
  • Transportation (vehicles, airplanes)
  • Agricultural activities (pesticides, fertilizers)
  • Natural sources (volcanoes, dust storms)

Effects of Air Pollution

  • Respiratory problems (asthma, bronchitis)
  • Cardiovascular diseases
  • Damage to ecosystems
  • Acid rain
  • Ozone depletion
  • Global warming

Solutions to Air Pollution

  • Reduce emissions (clean energy, public transportation)
  • Improve air quality standards
  • Promote sustainable practices (recycling, reducing waste)
  • Invest in renewable energy (solar, wind)
  • Plant trees and increase green spaces
