Rohit Sharma

Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian international cricketer and the current captain of the Indian national team in all three formats [20, 23]. He is a right-handed batsman and an occasional right-arm off-break bowler [20]. He plays for Mumbai in domestic cricket and captains Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League [20].

Career Highlights

  • Captain of the Indian national team in limited-overs formats [20]
  • Holds the record for the most sixes in ODI World Cup history [25]
  • Most hundreds in ODI World Cup history [25]
  • Scored three double centuries in One Day Internationals [24]

Personal Life

Rohit Sharma was born in Nagpur, Maharashtra, to a Telugu-Marathi-speaking family [23]. His mother, Purnima Sharma, is from Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh [23]. His father, Gurunath Sharma, worked as a caretaker at a transport firm storehouse [23]. He is married to Ritika Sajdeh [21].
