Hima Das

Hima Das is an Indian sprinter. She was born on January 9, 2000, in Dhing, Assam, India.

Personal Life

  • Birthdate: January 9, 2000
  • Birthplace: Dhing, Assam, India
  • Parents: Ronjit Das and Jonali Das
  • Siblings: One of five siblings
  • Education: Dhing Public High School

Sports Career

  • Sport: Track and Field
  • Events: 100m, 200m, 400m, 4x100m relay
  • Achievements:
    • First Indian woman to win a gold medal at the IAAF World U20 Championships (2018) [7]
    • Multiple gold medals at the Asian Games [6]
